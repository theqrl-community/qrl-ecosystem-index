QuantaSwap is a decentralized exchange (DEX) built on QRL 2.0 smart contracts. It provides an automated market maker (AMM) system allowing users to swap tokens, provide liquidity, and earn yields.

The protocol uses a constant product formula for price discovery, ensuring liquidity in all market conditions. Users can earn QSWAP tokens by providing liquidity to pool pairs.
